Callie is the Program Manager at the MIDS, where she is responsible for managing the Moodle platform, intensive and optional courses, and a variety of administrative matters and projects.

Prior to joining the MIDS, Callie worked for the Harvard Law School Graduate Program as Student Affairs Administrator & S.J.D. Program Coordinator. There, she managed a wide range of administrative matters in support of the doctoral program.

Callie holds a master's degree from the Harvard Graduate School of Education with a focus on higher education, as well as two bachelor's degrees (covering three subject areas: international education; French and francophone studies; English) from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
