The CIDS is dedicated to advancing pioneering research that tackles current challenges in international dispute settlement, enriching both academic discourse and practical application. Through interdisciplinary collaboration and engaging with leading experts, the CIDS generates impactful insights and develops innovative methodologies to enhance the effectiveness and fairness of international dispute resolution. The Center's research initiatives prioritize real-world application. We actively engage with key actors and institutions in international dispute resolution, aiming to influence policymaking and strengthen the global legal framework governing international disputes.


Research in law, and in particular criteria for excellence in research, have over the last decades globally shifted from a service towards legal practice to a purpose in itself, seeking the advancement of knowledge and understanding. CIDS in part follows this trend but, aware of the importance of continued critical contributions by academia to the defense and development of pacific methods of dispute settlement, it also significantly engages in service to practice.
