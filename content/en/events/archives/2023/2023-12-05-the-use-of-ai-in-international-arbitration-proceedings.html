Program

  • Welcome and introduction by Prof. Jacques de Werra, Director of the Digital Law Center
  • Presentation by Claire Morel de Westgaver, Partner at Bryan Cave Leighton Paisner

  • Benjamin Malek, Arbitrator at T.H.E. Chambers, Chair of the SVAMC AI Task Force
  • Prof. Pietro Ortolani, Full Professor of Digital Conflict Resolution at Radboud University, Nijmegen, the Netherlands.
