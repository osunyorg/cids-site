Recent and ongoing research projects on international dispute settlement undertaken by CIDS associated faculty and researchers.

header_text: >- header_cta: display: false label: >- target: "" external: false contents_reading_time: seconds: 18 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

Research in law, and in particular criteria for excellence in research, have over the last decades globally shifted from a service towards legal practice to a purpose in itself, seeking the advancement of knowledge and understanding. CIDS in part follows this trend but, aware of the importance of continued critical contributions by academia to the defense and development of pacific methods of dispute settlement, it also significantly engages in service to practice.
