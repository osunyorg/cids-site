The MIDS is launching a call for donations to go towards the annual MIDS Alumni Scholarship.

The MIDS Alumni Scholarship Fund was established in September 2018 and aims at providing financial support to one MIDS student each academic year.

Every gift, regardless of size, is a step towards enabling outstanding students to achieve their educational dreams with us. Your generosity helps open doors for deserving students with exceptional potential, ensuring that financial constraints don't stand in the way of their educational journey with us.
