The MIDS and Lambadarios Law Firm are delighted to welcome you to a one-day event in Athens for a conference on strategies and options to build a career in international arbitration and key trends in the development of investment law. The event is part of the Epaminondas Lambadarios Scholarship award launched in 2020 which sponsors a partial scholarship each year to support a student admitted to the MIDS program. Please find more details here. The event will feature a keynote speech from Zachary Douglas, KC as well as dedicated sessions on the following topics:

  • The MIDS program;
  • The in-house counsel perspective; and
  • Strategies and skills for pursuing a career in international arbitration

This interactive event will ensure participants have the opportunity to discuss with experienced practitioners and academics. A cocktail reception will follow at the same venue. 

You can download the full program here.

Keynote Speaker

Zachary Douglas, KC - Governance, Faculty

Professor, Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies; Matrix Chambers


Location

New Hotel, 16 Filellinon Street, Athens 10 557, Greece
