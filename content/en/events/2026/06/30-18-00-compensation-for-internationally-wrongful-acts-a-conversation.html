The Centre for International Dispute Settlement (CIDS) and the Geneva LL.M. in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS) are pleased to invite you to an evening conversation on compensation for damage caused by internationally wrongful acts, on the occasion of the commencement of the International Law Commission's (ILC) work on this topic, featuring:

Professor Mārtiņš Paparinskis
Professor of Public International Law, UCL; Member of the United Nations International Law Commission; Special Rapporteur on Compensation for the Damage Caused by Internationally Wrongful Acts.


Professor Patrícia Galvão Teles
Member of the United Nations International Law Commission; Member of the Permanent Court of Arbitration; Associate Professor of International Law, Universidade Autónoma de Lisboa.


Professor Zachary Douglas KC

Professor of International Law, Geneva Graduate Institute; MIDS Program Director; Director of the CIDS (Center for International Dispute Settlement), Barrister and King's Counsel at Three Verulam Buildings (3VB), London

Professor Mārtiņš Paparinskis
Professor of Public International Law, UCL; Member of the United Nations International Law Commission; Special Rapporteur on Compensation for the Damage Caused by Internationally Wrongful Acts.


Professor Patrícia Galvão Teles
Member of the United Nations International Law Commission; Member of the Permanent Court of Arbitration; Associate Professor of International Law, Universidade Autónoma de Lisboa.


Professor Zachary Douglas KC

Professor of International Law, Geneva Graduate Institute; MIDS Program Director; Director of the CIDS (Center for International Dispute Settlement), Barrister and King's Counsel at Three Verulam Buildings (3VB), London
