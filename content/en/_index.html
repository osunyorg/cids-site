The Geneva Center for International Dispute Settlement (CIDS) is a leading institution focused on the study and practice of international dispute resolution. A joint initiative of the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (IHEID) and the University of Geneva (UNIGE), the CIDS aims to advance the field through education, research, and policy analysis. Its mission is to promote excellence in the resolution of international disputes by providing top-tier educational programs, including the Master of Advanced Studies in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS) and specialized executive education. CIDS strives to create a global network of skilled professionals and scholars, contributing to the effective and fair resolution of disputes on the international stage.
