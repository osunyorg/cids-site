The CIDS is home to the flagship Geneva Masters's program (LL.M.) in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS), a unique, exclusive, and intensive one-year long academic program, bringing together academics and practitioners to train the dispute settlement lawyers of tomorrow.Â 

The MIDS offers a rigorous academic curriculum designed to equip students with deep theoretical knowledge and practical skills in arbitration and dispute resolution. In addition to the MIDS, CIDS provides executive education tailored for professionals seeking to enhance their expertise in this field. These programs underscore CIDS's commitment to fostering a global community of skilled practitioners capable of addressing complex international disputes effectively.
