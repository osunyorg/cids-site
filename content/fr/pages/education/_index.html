The CIDS is dedicated to advancing education in international dispute resolution through its comprehensive programs, including its flagship Master's program.

The MIDS

The MIDS (Master of Advanced Studies in International Dispute Settlement), offers a rigorous academic curriculum designed to equip students with deep theoretical knowledge and practical skills in arbitration and dispute resolution. In addition to the MIDS, CIDS provides executive education tailored for professionals seeking to enhance their expertise in this field. These programs underscore CIDS's commitment to fostering a global community of skilled practitioners capable of addressing complex international disputes effectively.


Learn more about the MIDS

https://mids.cids.osuny.site/en/
