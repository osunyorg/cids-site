The MIDS Alumni Association is delighted to welcome you to its 4th Annual Conference, which will revolve around four main topics:

- "We're losing the ISDS fight"? If so, what next?

- Navigating Corruption-related Claims in Commercial Arbitration

- Battle of the Landmark Decisions

- Combating Climate Change through International Law: Symbolism or Effective Change? 

Who is this event for?

All students, professors, as well as seasoned arbitration practitioners are encouraged to join us in these interesting discussions, either in-person or online. Attendance is free of charge but registration is required here. 

Voluntary contribution to the MIDS Alumni Scholarship

If you would like to help prospective students access the MIDS education, you can click this link to make a donation.
