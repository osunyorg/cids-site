The MIDS and Lambadarios Law Firm are delighted to welcome you to their Arbitration Conference that will take place on 18 October, as part of the 2023 Athens Arbitration Days.

The event is part of the Epaminondas Lambadarios Scholarship award launched in 2020 that sponsors a partial scholarship each year to support a student admitted to the MIDS program. Please find more details here.

With focus on strategies and options to build a career in international arbitration, as well as key international arbitration issues, the event will also feature a keynote speech from Zachary Douglas, KC.

The different panels of the conference will focus on the following topics:

  • Building a career in international dispute settlement - the MIDS program;
  • Advocacy and Witness Examination; and
  • Sharing experience with young arbitration practitioners

This interactive event will ensure participants have the opportunity to discuss with experienced practitioners and academics.

A cocktail reception will follow at the same venue. Further information may be found here. 

 

Keynote Speaker

Zachary Douglas, KC (external link)

Governance, Faculty

Professor, Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies; Matrix Chambers

 

Location

New Hotel, 16 Filellinon Street, 10 557
