Callie is the MIDS Program Manager, where she leads the student office team and oversees the various administrative and pedagogical aspects of the program to ensure its smooth delivery.

With over a decade of experience in higher education administration, Callie brings expertise from her previous role at Harvard Law School, where she served as the Student Affairs Administrator and S.J.D. Program Coordinator. In this capacity, she managed key administrative functions for both the doctoral (S.J.D.) and master's (LL.M.) programs, supporting international students throughout their academic journeys.

Callie holds a master's degree from the Harvard Graduate School of Education, specializing in higher education. She also earned dual bachelor's degrees from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, with concentrations in international education, French and Francophone studies, and English.
