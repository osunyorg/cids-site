In celebration of the 80th birthday of Professor Pierre Tercier, who taught for over 10 years at the Geneva LLM in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS), a group of his former employees and friends are organizing a special event on 12 May 2023. The event will gather arbitration practitioners, professionals and academics of his close entourage to celebrate his career and more in various entertaining formats, focusing on the following aspects:

  • The Person
  • The Professor
  • The Arbitrator
  • The ICC Court President
  • The Mentor

The event will include an extended coffee/tea break as well as follow-up reception to allow the participants ample time to catch up with Professor Tercier and each other.

You can download the full program here (external link).

Location

Auditorium A1-B
Maison de la Paix
Chemin Eugène-Rigot 2
Geneva
