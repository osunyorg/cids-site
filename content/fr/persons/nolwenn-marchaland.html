Nolwenn joined the MIDS team in November 2023 as MIDS Administrative Assistant, where she is responsible for the day-to-day administration and logistics required for the delivery of the MIDS Program. Prior to joining the MIDS, Nolwenn worked for six years as a Master's Programs Coordinator at Ecole polytechnique in France.

Nolwenn holds a Master's degree in European Politics from the University of Rennes.
