meta_description: >- summary: >- Join us for a forward-looking multi-stakeholder discussion about the essential practical skills that arbitration practitioners need in order to thrive in a technology & AI-driven future.

Organized by the MIDS and hosted by the ICC, this panel aims to help equip the participants (which should be of particular interest to young / upcoming practitioners) with actionable insights to navigate the opportunities and challenges ahead.

The session will also offer a privileged opportunity to learn more and discuss informally about the MIDS program for candidates.


Who is this event for?
MIDS Alumni, students, professors, as well as young arbitration practitioners are encouraged to join us for this interesting discussion at the ICC. Attendance is free of charge but registration is required.
