The Geneva Center for International Dispute Settlement (CIDS) is a joint center of the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (IHEID) and the University of Geneva (UNIGE), dedicated to the study of international dispute settlement. It is home to the flagship Geneva LL.M. in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS). 

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- The CIDS pillars slug: "title" position: 0 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Created by practitioners and academics committed to helping overcome today's challenges, we aim to create, interrogate, and disseminate knowledge on the law of international dispute settlement by focusing our activities on three pillars: education, research, and events.
