The MIDS and UNIGE Faculty of Law are delighted to welcome you to the Lecture with Professor Linos-Alexander Sicilianos, Former President of the European Court of Human Rights and Professor of International Law and Dean of the Faculty of Law of the University of Athens.

The Lecture will allow participants to address their questions either in English or in French.

A cocktail will follow the Lecture.

Professor Linos-Alexander Sicilianos is a Professor of International Law and the Dean of the Faculty of Law of the University of Athens. He is also a former Judge of the European Court of Human Rights (2011-2021). During his time at the Court, he was President of a Section of the Court in 2017; Vice-President of the Court from 2017 to 2019 and then President of the Court from 2019 to 2020.
