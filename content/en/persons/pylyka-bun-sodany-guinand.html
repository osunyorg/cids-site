Pylyka Bun Sodany Guinand joined the MIDS team in November 2021 as a MIDS Administrative Assistant. Before joining the MIDS, Pylyka worked for two years as a travel agent in Brussels and for six years as a Business Travel Consultant for the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) in Geneva. Pylyka organizes travel and events for the MIDS, in addition to assisting the MIDS Program Coordinator with various administrative tasks.
