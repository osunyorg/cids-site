The CIDS is dedicated to advancing education in international dispute resolution and fostering a global community of skilled practitioners through its comprehensive programs.

Our education and training programs are designed to equip professionals at all stages of their careers with practical skills and expertise in international dispute resolution, particularly through our flagship Master’s program, the MIDS.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- The MIDS slug: >- the-mids ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: alt_background text: >-

The Master of Advanced Studies (LL.M.) in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS), offers a rigorous academic curriculum designed to equip students with strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills in international dispute resolution.

notes: >- image: id: "f593e660-67b2-40d4-9988-a73f656e3e2c" file: "f593e660-67b2-40d4-9988-a73f656e3e2c" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: testimonials title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: testimonials: - text: >-

The MIDS was established 15 years ago as a bold attempt to create an innovative program catering to the specific needs of the practice of international dispute settlement. It's now trained more than 500 of the world's best lawyers and serves as a model in the field.
