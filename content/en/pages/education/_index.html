Education

The CIDS is dedicated to advancing education in international dispute resolution and fostering a global community of skilled practitioners through its comprehensive programs.Our education and training programs are designed to equip professionals at all stages of their careers with practical skills and expertise in international dispute resolution, particularly through our flagship Master’s program, the MIDS.

The MIDS

The Master of Advanced Studies (LL.M.) in International Dispute Settlement (MIDS), offers a rigorous academic curriculum designed to equip students with strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills in international dispute resolution.

"The MIDS was established in 2008 as a bold attempt to create an innovative program catering to the specific needs of the practice of international dispute settlement. It's now trained more than 500 of the world's best lawyers and serves as a model in the field."
Professor Zachary Douglas KC
CIDS Director, MIDS Program Director

Other education programs

In addition to the MIDS, CIDS provides executive education tailored for professionals seeking to enhance their expertise in the field.


There are no executive education courses currently open for registration.View our past course offerings below.
