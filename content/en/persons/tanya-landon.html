Tanya Landon is a trusted arbitrator in high-stakes international disputes.  She has over 20 years of experience at leading international law firms in Geneva and Paris, where she acted as counsel and arbitrator in a broad range of commercial and investment arbitrations across diverse industries and institutional rules. Focusing now on arbitrator work, Tanya brings to every dispute a combination of deep legal expertise, sound judgment, strong case management skills and an unwavering commitment to excellence, fairness and integrity.  

Tanya is admitted to the New York bar, is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, a member of the Executive Committee of the Geneva-based Foundation for International Arbitration Advocacy (FIAA) and serves on the Arbitration Court of the Swiss Arbitration Centre. A dual national of Canada and France with longstanding ties to Switzerland, she handles common law and civil law disputes in both English and French.  

Tanya is recognized by Chambers, Legal 500 and Lexology / Who's Who. Her peers state: "Tanya Landon is an excellent lawyer and arbitrator who combines good judgement with diligence and superior oral and written communication skills." (Chambers); and "Tanya Landon is highly intelligent, thoughtful and diligent.  She is unflappable in challenging situations and pragmatic in finding solutions." (Legal 500).
